## GENERATION TIPS FOR NANO BANANA

1. **Consistency:** Always start with the style prefix for city/game assets
2. **Transparent backgrounds:** Essential for compositing onto the board
3. **Resolution:** 
   - Cities & utilities: 512×512 (scaled to ~80×60 on tiles)
   - Central vault: 1024×1024 (displayed at ~300×300)
   - Card art: 512×720 (card proportions)
   - Coins/tokens: 256×256
4. **If animations are supported:** Add "subtle idle animation, slight bobbing motion, twinkling lights" to city prompts
5. **Batch by tier:** Generate all Green cities together, then Blue, etc. — keeps style consistent within tiers
